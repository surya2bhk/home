Wanna live in Badangpet? Real estate picks up pace

Boduppal and Badangpet municipalities in the city outskirts are seeing rise in real estate activities.

Posted by Surya Rayarao on August 17, 2019

Boduppal and Badangpet municipalities in the city outskirts are seeing rise in real estate activities. There has been a rise in the number of building permissions granted in the two municipalities, especially Boduppal. The numbers are higher than those in Karimnagar and other district headquarters such as Mancherial in the past two years. Boduppal ranked among the top five civic bodies that granted building permissions after Greater Hyderabad, Warangal and Khammam municipal corporations.

These areas were run by gram panchayats till two years ago and were given the status of municipality in 2016 by merging surrounding areas. Boduppal municipality was created by merging Boduppal, Chengicherla and Hemanagar.

1,600 permissions in Badangpet

According to official statistics, since July 2016, Boduppal municipality granted about 1,700 permissions, while Badangpet municipality permitted 1,600 constructions. In the same period, Karimnagar municipal corporation gave 1,500 permissions and 1,200 buildings were permitted in Mancherial.

"Boduppal civic body jurisdiction is close to Hyderabad-Warangal highway. Though its jurisdiction is very less, there has been rapid development in the area. Most importantly, we have arrested illegal constructions and ensured that all the builders and owners take permission. Stern action has been initiated against unauthorized constructions," R Upender Reddy, Boduppal municipal commissioner.

Source: Times of India
